				'Exceptions'              => 'exceptions',
				'Remarks'                 => 'remarks',
				'Description'             => 'description'
				'Validation'              => 'validation',

	    01b) $my_workspace & $my_project:
	         Appropriate Rally target Workspace and Project.

	    01c) $caliber_file_name and $caliber_image_directory:
	         The 2 input files containing data.
